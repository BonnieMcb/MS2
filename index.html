British Wildlife Memory Game

The British Wildlife Memory Game was created as a fun way to learn a bit more about British Wildlife.

Follow the links at the bottom of the page to learn more, or support the organisations that are involved in the conservation of the different species groups.

How to play

  • The tiles consist of pairs of images.
  • Find all the matching pairs by turning over the tiles.
  • Click on a tile to turn it over and reveal its picture.
  • Only 2 tiles can be turned over at once. You will have to remember where the pictures are to find the pairs.
